About MOURYA HOTEL| PATNA

Located in the heart of Patna adjacent to the city’s famous landmark Gandhi Maidan, Hotel Maurya Patna offers easy access to business districts, government offices and leisure attractions. A convenient 20-minutes drive from the airport or 10-minutes from the railway station will reach you at the destination. And once here, Maurya, a legacy hotel of over forty years, will welcome you with warm hospitality as well as an assurance that your stay in Patna is comfortable and memorable.

Patna, the erstwhile City of Pataliputra lies to the south of the Ganges River. This ancient city has seen the rise & fall of many empires, and has served as the centre of culture, spirituality, politics and commerce for many years. Since Independence, Patna has been the state capital of Bihar. On the outskirts of Patna and explorable as day tours are the Buddhist, Hindu and Jain pilgrimage centres of Vaishali, Rajgir, Nalanda, Bodh Gaya and Pawapuri. Patna City is also a sacred city for the Sikhs, it is here that the tenth Sikh Guru, Guru Gobind Singh was born. Patna is accessible by road (National Highway 30), it is also on the main line of the Eastern Railway and has an international airport with select flights to South-East Asia.
