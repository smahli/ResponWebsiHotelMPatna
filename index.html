Hotel Maurya Patna is undoubtedly one of the most celebrated landmarks in Bihar. From the time it first opened its doors in 1978 to welcome Patna residents and international tourists, it has remained an iconic hospitality venue. Maurya is Bihar's first luxury's hotel in the state.

The History Of Our Hotel

Patna, the erstwhile City of Pataliputra lies to the south of the Ganges River. This ancient city has seen the rise & fall of many empires, and has served as the centre of culture, spirituality, politics and commerce for many years. Since Independence, Patna has been the state capital of Bihar. On the outskirts of Patna and explorable as day tours are the Buddhist, Hindu and Jain pilgrimage centres of Vaishali, Rajgir, Nalanda, Bodh Gaya and Pawapuri.
